Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our services

How long does it take to complete a project?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity and scope. A simple website might take 2-4 weeks, while a complex web application could take 3-6 months. During our initial consultation, we'll provide a detailed timeline based on your specific requirements.

Do you provide ongoing maintenance?

Yes, we offer various maintenance packages to keep your website or application secure, up-to-date, and performing optimally. Our maintenance services include regular updates, security patches, performance monitoring, and content updates. We can customize a maintenance plan to suit your specific needs.

What is your payment process?

We typically work with a 50% upfront deposit to begin the project, with the remaining balance due upon completion. For larger projects, we may structure payments into milestones. We accept payments via bank transfer, credit card, or PayPal. All payment terms are clearly outlined in our contract before work begins.

Do you work with clients internationally?

Absolutely! We work with clients from around the world. Our team is experienced in managing remote projects effectively through clear communication, regular updates, and collaborative tools. We adjust our working hours when needed to accommodate different time zones for meetings and discussions.

What technologies do you work with?

We work with a wide range of technologies, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Vue.js, Angular, Node.js, PHP, Python, WordPress, Shopify, and many others. We choose the most appropriate technology stack for each project based on requirements, scalability needs, and long-term maintenance considerations.

How do you handle project changes?

We understand that project requirements can evolve during development. Minor changes are typically accommodated within the project scope. For more significant changes, we'll discuss the impact on timeline and budget before proceeding. We document all change requests and ensure clear communication throughout the process.
